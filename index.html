The Collections
925 Sterling Silver
Real metal. Real hallmark. Worn without rules. Hallmarked — tarnish-resistant with care. Built to be worn, not stored. Finishes: Rhodium · Natural Silver.
Vermeil
Gold on the outside. Silver at the core. 925 Sterling Silver base with thick gold plating (min. 2.5 microns). Not brass. Never called "gold-plated" without context. Finishes: 14K Yellow Gold · 18K Yellow Gold · Rose Gold (Limited Edition).
Charms & Modular Pieces
Start with one. Build your collection. Every charm is designed to connect — to each other, to base bracelets, to the version of you that's slowly building something beautiful. Eumi's most loyal product.
Rose Gold — Limited Edition ✨
Rose Gold is Eumi's most coveted finish — and its scarcity is intentional. It drops, it sells, it's gone. Never use "Out of Stock" — use "This drop has ended." The gap is the point. Always vermeil. Always limited.
Materials Philosophy
Eumi works in two precious materials — both grounded in real silver, both designed to be worn, not saved.

925 Sterling Silver — Hallmarked precious metal. The backbone of Eumi — honest, beautiful, and built to last. Every piece is stamped.

Vermeil — 925 Sterling Silver base with gold plating (minimum 2.5 microns). This is Eumi's gold — not brass, not plated base metal. Real silver underneath, always.

We do not work with base metals. Every Eumi piece starts with silver — because that's the only foundation worth building on.

THE MUSE COLLECTION

Materials Philosophy
14K / 18K / 22K GOLD VERMEIL
The entry point into the Caziél world. A thick layer of gold bonded to a fine metal base — carrying the warmth and colour of solid gold at a considered price point. Min. 2.5 micron gold layer.
SOLID 14K / 18K / 21K / 22K GOLD
The material of heirlooms. Hallmarked, weighty, built to appreciate in value alongside the life of the wearer. 21K offered for Middle East and South Asian markets where high-purity gold is the cultural standard.
PLATINUM (Pt950) — FLAGSHIP ONLY
Reserved for the Caziél Flagship tier. Cool, rare, and indestructible — platinum carries a weight and permanence that no other metal can match. The metal of legacy.
We do not use silver or gold-plated base metal. Every Caziél creation is hallmarked and traceable.
